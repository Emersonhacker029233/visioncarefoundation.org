Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to eliminating preventable blindness worldwide

OUR MISSION

To spearhead the elimination of preventable and curable blindness by 2040 through the provision of affordable, accessible, sustainable, inclusive and quality eye health innovative solutions.

Vision Care Foundation aims to create a world where everyone has access to quality eye care regardless of their economic status or geographic location. We are committed to developing sustainable solutions that address the root causes of vision impairment in underserved communities.

OUR VISION

A world where preventable blindness is eliminated and everyone has access to quality vision care services that enhance their quality of life and productivity.

We envision communities where eye health is prioritized, where no child struggles in school due to uncorrected vision, and where seniors maintain their independence through proper eye care. Our vision extends beyond treatment to prevention and education.

Our Core Values

The fundamental beliefs that guide our actions and decisions

Compassion

We approach every individual with empathy and understanding, recognizing the profound impact vision has on quality of life.

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards in all our programs, services, and partnerships.

Sustainability

We develop solutions that create lasting impact, empowering communities to maintain eye health independently.

Inclusivity

We serve all individuals regardless of age, gender, ethnicity, or socioeconomic status.

Why Choose Vision Care Foundation

What sets us apart in the field of vision care and blindness prevention

Comprehensive Approach

We address eye health from multiple angles - prevention, treatment, education, and advocacy - creating holistic solutions.

Local Empowerment

We train local healthcare workers and establish sustainable vision centers that continue serving communities long after our initial intervention.

Proven Impact

With over 50,000 vision screenings and 5,000 sight-restoring surgeries performed, our methods deliver measurable results.

Innovative Solutions

We develop and implement cutting-edge approaches to eye care delivery in low-resource settings.

Committed to eliminating preventable and curable blindness by 2040 through innovative, accessible, and sustainable eye health solutions.
