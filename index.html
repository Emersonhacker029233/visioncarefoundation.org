Our Comprehensive Approach

The Vision Care Foundation is dedicated to eliminating preventable and curable blindness by 2040 through a multifaceted strategy that combines innovation, accessibility, and sustainability. Our mission drives us to develop solutions that address the root causes of vision impairment in underserved communities.

We believe that clear vision is a fundamental human right, and our work focuses on creating systemic change in eye health delivery through:

  • Community-Based Screening: Mobile eye clinics reaching remote areas with free examinations
  • Affordable Treatment: Subsidized glasses, medications, and vision-saving procedures
  • Capacity Building: Training local healthcare workers in primary eye care techniques
  • Infrastructure Development: Establishing permanent vision centers in underserved regions
  • Research & Innovation: Developing new technologies to improve diagnosis and treatment
  • Advocacy: Influencing policy to prioritize eye health in public health agendas
