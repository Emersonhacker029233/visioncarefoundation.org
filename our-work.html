Our Projects

We implement sustainable solutions to improve eye health in Tanzania through innovative programs

School Based Eye Health Services

Arusha, Singida, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania

Providing comprehensive eye health services in primary and secondary schools, including refractive error screening and treatment, school vision corridors, and strengthening school eye health systems.

Community Eye Health Services

National Level, Tanzania

Delivering accessible eye care services to communities across Tanzania, with annual expansion based on targets and approved scope.

African Creativity for Eyesight (ACE)

Tanzania

A research project developing and validating free-hand animation and voice drama to improve eye health-seeking behavior, vision and wellbeing through community-involved strategies.
